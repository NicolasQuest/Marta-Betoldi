Contracciones

Synopsis

The monologues of two pregnant women, developed in two different times but united by the same circumstances of gestation, slowly weave the story of a mother and her daughter born in captivity. The symmetry and knots between both dialogues reveal the true identity. My contribution to this project focuses on sound editing.

Premiered at Sala El Nudo, as part of the Teatroxlaidentidad series. She was involved in its management along with other artists, supporting the work of Abuelas de Plaza de Mayo. The play was restaged in its full version outside the TxI cycle in Buenos Aires and on tours in Spain, running for two months at the Teatro de La Villa in Madrid. It was selected to participate in the Festival Teatro a Mil in Chile; Festival de Cádiz, Spain; Miami Festival in the USA. It was translated into 4 languages and premiered in Paris, London, Madrid, Mexico, and the USA.
