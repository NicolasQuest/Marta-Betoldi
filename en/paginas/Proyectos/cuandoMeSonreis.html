Cuando me Sonreís

Synopsis

"Cuando me sonreís" is an Argentine series that, in a romantic comedy tone, tells the love story between Gastón Murphy, an obsessive, solitary man who has chosen to remain single, and Luna Rivas, a disorganized, chaotic, and infatuated woman who turns her life upside down in five minutes.

Notable Recognitions

Nominated for Best Comedy at the Martín Fierro Awards
