Ciega a Citas

Synopsis

"Ciega a citas" tells the story of Lucía, who, at over 30 years old, is single, dislikes her job as a journalist, is overweight, and has a younger sister who is not only perfect but is about to get married. When her mother announces the news, she makes a bet with Irina: If Lucía doesn't show up alone, overweight, and dressed in black to the wedding, her mother will pay for the entire party. The protagonist sees this as a challenge and races against time: she has 258 days to find "a normal boyfriend" and will do everything possible to win the bet. What she doesn't know is that in the course of her search, she will find her true love, who has always been just 10 centimeters away. Starring Muriel Santana, Rafael Ferro, Georgina Barbarrosa, and a great cast. This award-winning series has been adapted in Spain, Germany, Hungary, China, and Chile with great success in the global market.
