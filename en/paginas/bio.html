Marta Betoldi is an Argentine actress, screenwriter, and playwright in television, theater, and cinema, residing in Madrid. She has worked as a screenwriter for series such as Socias, Ciega a citas, Cuando me Sonreís, Daños Colaterales, Edha, Esperanza mía, Solamente vos, Las Estrellas, El Host II, La Villa, Planners II, and Escribiendo el Fin del Mundo.

Currently, she is the head writer for the adaptation of Fernando Gaitán's original series "Betty la Fea 20 años después" for Amazon Global. Additionally, she is developing an original series for Netflix LATAM, serving as the Content Development Coordinator for Gosua España, providing script consultancy for Hispanic-American production companies, and serving as a member of the jury for the International Emmy Awards.
