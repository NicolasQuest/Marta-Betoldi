Marta Betoldi is an Argentine actress, scriptwriter, and playwright based in Madrid. She is the creator of series such as Socias, Ciega a Citas, Cuando me Sonreís, Daños Colaterales, Edha, Esperanza Mía, Solamente vos, Las Estrellas, El Host II, La Villa, Planners II, and Escribiendo el Fin del Mundo.

Creator of "Betty la Fea, 20 años después," a spin-off series based on the homonymous work written by Fernando Gaitán and head writer of the writing team. She is developing an original series for Netflix. She also serves as Content Development Coordinator for Gosua España and provides script doctoring services for Hispanic-American production companies. She is a member of the jury for the International Emmy Awards.
